Creating Your Account

Step-by-step guide to getting started on {{ project_detail.title }}.

1. Choose Your Role

From the home page, click the Join Us button in the navigation bar. You’ll be asked to choose whether you are signing up as a Gamer or Arena Owner.

  • Gamers can join competitions, manage profiles, and connect with shops.
  • Arena Owners can create arenas, manage tournaments, and track gamers.

2. Complete the Registration Form

Enter your basic details including name, email address, and a secure password that meets the platforms requirements.

  • Use an email address you have access to.
  • Choose a unique username that represents you.
  • Create a strong password with letters, numbers, and symbols.

3. Verify Your Email

After submitting the registration form, youll receive a verification email.

  • Open the email titled Verify Your Account.
  • Click the verification link to activate your account.
  • If you dont see it, check your spam or junk folder.

4. Complete Your Profile

Once verified, log in and complete your profile details so that other players and shops can discover you.

  • Add your preferred platforms and games.
  • Set a profile picture and short bio.
  • Review your privacy and notification settings.
