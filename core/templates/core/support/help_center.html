Technical Support

Connection & Performance

If you experience issues loading pages or joining matches, start with these checks:

  • Confirm you have a stable internet connection.
  • Try refreshing the page or using a different browser.
  • Disable VPNs or firewalls that may block game traffic.

If issues persist, reach out through the Contact Us page with as many details as possible.

Billing & Payments

Entry Fees & Refunds

Tournament entry fees and prize payouts follow the rules in our Terms & Conditions.

  • Most entry fees are non-refundable once a tournament starts.
  • Cancelled events are typically refunded automatically.
  • Review the Terms & Conditions for full details.
