Navigating the Platform

Learn how to move around {{ project_detail.title }} efficiently.

1. Main Navigation Bar

The top navigation bar gives you quick access to the most important areas of the platform.

  • Home  Overview of featured games, events, and stats.
  • About  Learn more about the platform and community.
  • Competitions  Browse and join upcoming tournaments.

2. Dashboards

Once logged in, your dashboard changes based on your role.

  • Gamer Dashboard  Track competitions, communities, and your stats.
  • Arena Owner Dashboard ߛ Manage arenas, tournaments, and registrations.

3. Accessing Settings

You can access your account and profile settings from your dashboard.

  • Open the menu from your dashboard header.
  • Click on Settings to manage security, notifications, and more.
  • Review these regularly to keep your account up to date.
