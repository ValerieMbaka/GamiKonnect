Joining Your First Tournament

A simple walkthrough for entering your first competition.

1. Find a Tournament

Go to the Competitions section from the navigation bar or your dashboard.

  • Browse available tournaments by game, platform, or date.
  • Open a tournament to view detailed rules and prize information.

2. Check Eligibility & Rules

Before joining, make sure the tournament fits your skill level and platform.

  • Confirm the required game version and platform (PC, Console, or Mobile).
  • Review entry fees and prize distribution details.
  • Read all rules to avoid disqualification.

3. Join and Prepare

Click the Join button on the tournament page, complete any required steps, and get ready to play.

  • Ensure your account details and in-game IDs are correct.
  • Be online and ready at the scheduled start time.
  • Watch for notifications with match details or lobby codes.
